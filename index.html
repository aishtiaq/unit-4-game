Crystal Collector!

Rules

A random number has been displayed at the top of the crystals.

There are 4 crystals. By clicking the crystals, you will add a number to your scrore.

You win the game by matching your score to the number. You lose if you go over.

The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.

The value of the crystals and the random number changes with each round.
